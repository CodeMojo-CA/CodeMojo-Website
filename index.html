Services

We create bespoke software from "soup to nuts". Drawing on 15 years experience delivering projects for such companies as Boeing, Sun Life, American Express, Encana and Husky, we know what it takes to complete a software project on time and on budget.

UX

Dress poorly and they notice the dress. Dress well and they notice the woman. So said Coco Channel. The same goes for your project. You want the users to notice that the design is intuitive, that it takes little time to get to the most important features, that you look after their needs and keep them safe. Our User eXperience designer will make sure that they feel that way.

Code

The goal of every project delivery effort should be that the code is clean and it works. Clean means that it doesn't require immediate refactoring, that it is properly unit tested, that the defect rate is industry beating. Works means not only that it functions well, but that it delivers functions. Your product manager and customer alike should beam with pride in how well it delivers the required functionality; that there is a 1 to 1 match between what was asked and what was delivered

Project Management

We get it. We all have to meet schedules. We are an agile shop. We follow the principles of agile software delivery. That gives us the ability to deliver precise estimates. Precise estimates allow you to plan...allow us to plan. Tell us what you need doing, and we'll build a plan to get you there, and then report on the progess using artefacts that make sense to you.

Technical Writing

More and more technical writing is not creating manual for your software. The users should be able to figure out how to use your application with little coaching. More and more technical writing is covering your bases for regulatory and security reasons. Whatever the reason, we'll make sure that what we write is on the money, and easy to understand
